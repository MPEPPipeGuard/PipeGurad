System Maintenance Guide

Weekly Tasks

  • Clean all sensors from dust and dirt
  • Check cables and seal integrity
  • Perform connectivity and alert tests
  • Verify solar charging station operation

Monthly Tasks

  • Oil the ultrasonic ring components
  • Inspect protective enclosures
  • Review system logs for anomalies
  • Test backup power systems

Yearly Tasks

  • Clean internal parts thoroughly
  • Replace damaged components
  • Check battery health and sensor precision
  • Calibrate all sensors to factory standards

Maintenance Tips

Always power down the system before performing maintenance. Keep a log of all maintenance activities including dates, tasks performed, and any observations. For complex issues, contact PipeGuard support for assistance.

Intervention Guidelines

Critical Leak

  • Immediate shutdown of affected section
  • Dispatch emergency response team
  • Notify all relevant stakeholders
  • Document incident with photos and logs

Minor Anomaly

  • Review within 24 hours
  • Schedule inspection if needed
  • Monitor for changes in status
  • Update maintenance records

Normal Operation

  • No immediate action required
  • Continue routine monitoring
  • Perform scheduled maintenance
  • Document system health status
